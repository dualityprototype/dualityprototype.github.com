Duality

A 3rd person, puzzle-adventure game developed with UDK.
Play as Catalyst to stop Anti-matter from negating the Matter universe


Winner of the Jury's Special Award & Nominated for Best Technical Innovation



@ 2013 Academia by Ubisoft Game Lab Competition

The Game

Duality started as McGill University's representative to the 2013 Ubisoft Academia Game Lab Competition. The game immerses the player into subatomic space where Antimatter must be stopped before it's too late. Particles, elements, dark matter, dark energy, electrons, neutrons, atoms, moles, isotopes and more can all be found in our visionary atomic universe!
